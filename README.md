# Sales-Performance-and-Operations-Dashboard

# Overview
This project demonstrates how Power BI dashboards can be used to analyse sales performance, customer ordering behaviour, and operational patterns for a food business.

Using Power BI and DAX, I built an interactive dashboard that highlights:

- Revenue drivers

- Peak ordering times

- Best-performing products and categories

- Opportunities to optimise staffing, promotions, and menu strategy

The dashboard is designed for business owners and operations managers to support day-to-day and strategic decisions.

# Business Problem

Food and QSR businesses often struggle with:

- Staffing inefficiencies during peak and off-peak hours

- Limited visibility into best-selling items and categories

- Missed revenue opportunities during low-demand periods

- Difficulty translating raw order data into operational actions

Key questions addressed:

- When do customers place the most orders?

- Which products drive the highest revenue and volume?

- Which days and times require higher staffing levels?

- What is the average value of an order?

- Increase staffing during afternoon and evening peak hours, especially Fridays

- Launch targeted promotions during low-demand periods (early mornings, late nights)

- Highlight top-performing pizzas in menus and marketing campaigns

- Bundle popular pizzas with sides or drinks to lift AOV

- Use time-based insights to improve operational efficiency
